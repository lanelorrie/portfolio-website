Objective:

Build a messenger app reusing designed UI components like: Layer Styles, Buttons and Character Styles.

Goal:

To create an app using my unique UI Kit for Peep. Learning how to streamline my
work by reusing components like character styles, buttons and colors.

MOODBOARD

Starting off I thought about what I wanted to name the app and what I wanted it to look like.
I got a lot of inspiration from my little yellow canary Poppy.

THE LOGO

I decided I wanted to dive deeper into why my bird Poppy inspired me for this project.
I started thinking about synonyms for words like “chat” and “talk.”
Then I thought about a popular slang for when people in the South say things like “I haven’t
heard a peep” when referring to whether or not they’ve heard from someone and voila!
Then I tied it back into the inspiration from my bird Poppy and the sounds and chirps she makes.
Thus peep was born!

STYLEGUIDE

HIGH-FIDELITY SCREEN MOCKUPS

Using my UI Kit I created just for Peep I then started to design the 4 screens for this project.
I wanted to make it simple and show the most common screens a user would interact with.
The splash screen, the chat screen, the users contact list and their active chats.
