About Me

My Design Journey

Hello, I’m Lorrie Lane. A Graphic Designer turned UI/UX Designer with a passion for Reptiles, Birds and all things design!

Working for a newspaper company designing dozens of ads daily for various papers and clients has taught me a lot about what it means to design for the client in mind. Now I am looking forward to taking all I’ve learned and applying it to web and mobile app design. My experience as a Graphic Designer has allowed me to seamlessly transition these skills into the digital design world. I’m ready to make a difference in an innovative team that places the user first to make products that are comfortable to use and visually stunning.

I enjoy learning front-end development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript). I also enjoy frequently attending design-related online events within the community. I also participate in UI challenges to keep my skills fresh, improve and also keep up with trends!
